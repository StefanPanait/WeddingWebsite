Hi Cutie!

Wendy and Stefan’s different cultural backgrounds allow for a modern twist on wedding celebrations.

Dress code: Semi formal / festive attire Traditionally, red plays a vital role in Chinese weddings, because this color is associated with success, loyalty, honor, fertility, and love, amongst others for the couple. Guests are encouraged to wear bright colours such as peach, pink, and lavender hues to symbolize the birth of a new life the couple is embarking on together. Generally, red, white, and black are best avoided for the guest. For the wedding day, Wendy will be wearing a Western style wedding gown for the ceremony and a traditional Chinese red dress for the reception.
